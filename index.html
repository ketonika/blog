Published October 10, 2021
by Manila Bulletin Entertainment
Charlie Dizon

Charlie Dizon has made truly amazing progress in her career. At the age of 25 she already has legendary photosets and an award winning movie role in “Soldier’s Heart” under her belt. Being an ambassador for a major cosmetics brand is also not a small thing to achieve at this age. However, nothing is free in this world. Working so hard, Charlie Dizon forgot to take care of herself. Her body was not as forgiving and responded with numerous health problems caused by stress. Charlie Dizon was only 18 at that time. The world of show business does not like to see pain on its models’ faces. The management was not much help either. To ensure the contract obligations are fulfilled, they gave all sorts of harmful quick-fix medications to young Charlie Dizon without even consulting the doctors because of the time restrictions. Obviously, this led to more problems and to eventual hospitalization of Charlie Dizon with liver and skin problems.

Being at the hospital should have made the situation even worse for Charlie’s career. Having to stay in bed meant she put on a lot of weight. Usually, directors and producers are not happy to see their lead actress overweight. Still though, Charlie turned this event to her advantage. It is exactly because of her new “body type” she managed to land a role in “Soldier’s Heart”, which brought her to a new level of success and recognition. However, there was still a problem of excessive weight. The opportunities like she used to find are not numerous and a typical “modeling” body type was still a must-have. Anyone who went to a gym trying to lose weight will tell you this is not the fastest way to get slimmer.

Charlie Dizon does not have a metabolism of a Ferrari engine and never did any extreme drugs. Thus she had to look for non-trivial methods of weight loss. Luckily, on one of the forums. She discovered Ketonica, a way to lose weight naturally without using illegal substances or extreme fasting. The results it promised seemed nothing short of a miracle and Charlie Dizon was suspicious about high promises already. Yet, after doing research, she found out that Ketonica contained only natural ingredients that did not change human metabolism but launched a natural process similar to what Keto diet does. The only difference is that it does not require months of strict dieting.

The progress went just the way it was promised. Charlie took Ketonica according to instructions and the kilograms melted. What is more surprising, Charlie did not noticed any major differences in the way she felt. Usually, quick weight loss was accompanied by apathy and lack of energy. While taking Ketonica, Charlie felt a lot better than usual. A sort of second breath opened for her and she became even more energetic, surprising people with her determination. The numbers also were not lying as the kilograms steadily decreased and for many months now, Charlie maintains stable weight.

“I call it a Keto-diet Express” she laughs. “Because I literally practiced it while being on my way between the filming locations and because it is really fast and safe.” Charlie got back in shape right before the filming had to start.
